# 🌱 Digital Life Lessons

A modern full-stack web application where users can create, organize, preserve, and share meaningful life lessons, personal growth insights, and wisdom gathered throughout their lives.

The platform allows users to document experiences, learn from others, save favorite lessons, engage through comments and reactions, and unlock Premium features through Stripe-powered subscriptions.

---

## 🔗 Live Website

**Live Site:** https://digital-life-lessons-client-psi.vercel.app

---

## 📌 Project Purpose

People learn valuable lessons throughout their lives but often forget them over time.

**Digital Life Lessons** provides a platform where users can preserve personal wisdom, reflect on their growth, and inspire others by sharing meaningful life experiences. The platform encourages mindful learning and community-driven growth.
